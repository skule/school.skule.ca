Logos, Patches, and Icons

Throughout the years, I've been asked to help design logos for various school clubs and other groups. I've also designed patches and other personal projects.

Logos

A logo designed for Lock Bypass Village, a group that runs workshops and other events about physical security.

A logo designed for a group in the University of Toronto Engineering Orientation 2018. These were later made into keepsakes for the first years.

A logo designed for Skule Juggling Club, a student-run undergraduate club. This logo was made as a favour to the head of the juggling club.

A logo designed for Skule Dungeons and Dragons Club, a student-run undergraduate club.

Patches

A patch parodying the popular video game 'Untitled Goose Game', featuring the University of Toronto Engineering mascot and other important figures.
