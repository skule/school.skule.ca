Social Media

I have created posts for and run the social media accounts of a variety of school clubs and groups, creating everything from announcements to information posts for Facebook and Instagram.

As the Graphics and Social Media executive for a number of undergraduate clubs and organizations, I've created a variety of online posts to announce major events, and to inform other students about groups, clubs, and events happening around campus. In this position, I had to ensure that posts were attention-grabbing, aesthetically pleasing, and easy to read on a mobile device, since we were prioritizing Instagram as our primary method of posting.
