Corporate Branding

While working for a local Toronto startup, I was asked to create promotional materials, document and slideshow templates, and other items for the company to use to have a consistent brand across their files.

I was asked to create a layout for what would be used for a variety of documents, including On-Site Reports and information booklets about available services. I designed for them two different title pages as well as a corresponding main text layout consistent with their colour scheme. I also helped them edit their services document layout to create a more visually appealing document and helped proofread and edit the text itself.

In addition to this, I put together a website for the company, with a variety of custom page layouts and designs, again using the company's colour scheme in an aesthetically pleasing way without sacrificing readability or becoming distracting to the viewer.
