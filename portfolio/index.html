Intro

Graphic Design Portfolio

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm Karen Ng, a Toronto-based graphic designer looking for work. Whether it's logos for companies and groups, social media posts, or even app and web design, if it needs graphic designing, I'm more than happy to work on it.

As a computer engineering student at the University of Toronto, I am a quick learner in up-to-the-minute technology, and am used to working in a fast paced environment. I love incorporating new tech and ideas into my work, both in the world of computer engineering and the world of graphic design.

In my free time, I enjoy working on 3D printing projects, drawing and painting, and working backstage at my local theatre.
